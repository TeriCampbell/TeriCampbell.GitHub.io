Design noteBoth pre-select selections and free text feed into the model together. The model receives the full prior plan, check-in history, and update inputs and regenerates both the full scope view and the rolling seven-day view. Student does not re-enter previously captured information. Free text field is hidden entirely for flagged users with no placeholder or explanation. Pre-selects carry the full weight of the interaction for flagged users. All free text screened on submission.
