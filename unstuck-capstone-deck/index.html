Why this problem is worth solving now

  • High school students juggle many deadlines. Overwhelm can escalate quickly once they feel behind.
  • Unstuck addresses the paralysis around where to start with one clear next action.
  • Plans include resource links and context, so students can find the right support later.
  • Adult support links can trigger account setup help or counselor check-ins when needed.

Student path

School path

Counselor dashboard turns session, plan, check-in, and event data into anonymized aggregate patterns.

  • At 372:1, counselors cannot give every student a personalized “where to start” conversation. Unstuck extends that guidance between appointments with structured plans students can act on without another 1:1 meeting.
  • Dashboard: actionable anonymized category trends and engagement patterns support targeted strategies to address gaps in performance for metrics that leaders are accountable for, such as test performance, course completion, and attendance.
  • Why schools pay: intervene sooner across the caseload, align support with regional priorities, and adopt with school-level accountability instead of an informal app recommendation
